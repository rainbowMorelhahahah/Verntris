    • ABOUT VENTRIS

    Ventris represents true luxury and exclusivity that embodies design, technology, craftsmanship and practicality. Originated from the USA and made in Japan, Ventris unveils its very first La Corsa suitcase series with the brand’s proprietary handcrafted V-shaped carbon fiber outer shell design. As the icon of top luxury lifestyle brand in the world, Ventris puts forward a distinctive perception of luxury travel and vital lifestyle to global jet-setters.

    Inspired by the high life and a passion for refinement and travel, a group of passionate entrepreneurs infuse world travel, car racing and a zest of luxury with functionality, an idea like no other. They co-found Ventris to revolutionize the modern luxury travel experience by inventing full carbon fiber luggage that are invincible, timeless and uncover the one-of-a-kind style

    CRAFTSMANSHIP WITH
    HANDS AND HEART

    manufacturer of carbon fibers, each carbon fiber luggage is crafted individually by hands from molding to polishing, in a specialized production process similar to that used in the creation of high-performance race cars, supercars and aircrafts

    Ventris crafts its iconic V as a unique design that is both aesthetically and functionally appealing, arousing world travelers with distinctive sense of refinement and creating a crave for sophisticated details of the symmetrical V-shaped carbon fiber pattern.

    The masterly craft production involves skillful handicraft that is far beyond your imagination. Embracing the art of symmetry, the carbon fiber sheets must be precisely cut and perfectly merged by hands to form a flawless symmetrical V shape that cannot be created by any machines. It’s then carefully placed in a special mold layer by layer by Ventris master artisans – at least 3 layers – and baked in a large oven to accomplish the finest craftsmanship that reveals the right balance between luxury, strength, and design

    VENTRIS QUALITY TEST

    At Ventris, we put each of our suitcases through a series of quality tests according to the international standards to assure their peak performance wherever you explore. Ventris quality policy expresses our commitment and vision to deliver the finest product and customer service, providing our customers with value added travel solutions.
